Therapeutic WebAR Biofeedback Research System

AR-assisted therapy,
driven by your real heartbeat

A final-year research project exploring real-time biofeedback through augmented reality. The system reads live heart rate data and responds with clinically-informed visual environments designed to reduce acute stress.

Live biofeedback loop HypeRate watch → Node.js server → AR scene, updated in real time via Socket.IO
EBD prescription engine Heart rate and HRV drive colour, particle density and mode transitions
Session outcome reporting Baseline, peak, ending BPM and clinical interpretation exportable as PDF
